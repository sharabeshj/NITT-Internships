About Us

Education is the premise of progress, in every society, in every family.

Kofi Annan

In today's ever-changing and dynamic environment, Higher education can be seen as a focal point of knowledge and its application. We at R.E.I, or the Research Experience Initiative, aim to establish ourselves and contribute to growth and development by fostering innovation and increasing higher order research skills, described as key drivers of growth performance, prosperity and competitiveness.
We seek to provide the exposure, the budding students need to raise their standards and nurture scientific temper, gearing them for the arduous journey that is Graduate school and beyond. The initiative aims to participate and invest in knowledge creation and enabling scientific skill development through mandated research programs as the key to enhancing productivity and growth, for a better tomorrow!
